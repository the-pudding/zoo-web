Last updated

Has 2020 got you down?

Need a little cute animal therapy?


We’ve got you.

Once an hour, we generate short clips from live-streaming webcams of animals in zoos and aquariums worldwide. We’ve collected all of those clips here to create a digital zoo.

Like physical zoos, ours is arranged by the animals’ natural habitat. As you scroll, you’ll travel from the arctic polar bears to the Antarctic penguins with many other animals in between.

Click on any video clip for more information about the animals, the facility, how to donate to those animals and more.
